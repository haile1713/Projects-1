OUR HISTORY

Nimble is a British multinational auto maker that has its main head quarter in London. It was founded on July 18,1994. the company sells automobiles and commercial vehicles under Nimble brand and most luxury cars, Nimble also has a joint-ventures in china(changan Nimble), Taiwan, America and Russia. Nimble introduced new form of large scale manufacture of cars and large scale management of an industrial work force. It has a relationship with ford company and also owned the former UK subsidiaries jaguar and land rover of ford company. Nimble is the third largest US based automaker( behind ford motors) and 6th largest in world (behind Toyota, Volkswagen, Hyundai-kia and ford) based on 2015 vehicle production. The company has no yet went public as wanted because of the financial crises at the beginning of 21st century, it was nearly close to bankruptcy, but it has since returned to profitability. In 2008 Nimble produced 5.323 million automobiles and employed about 222,303 employees at around 80 plants and facilities worldwide. Nowadays Nimble has more than 10 branches around the world and has over a million of employees also manufactured a number of vehicles.
